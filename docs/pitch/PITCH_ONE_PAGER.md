# Watchify — pitch one-pager

**Product:** Watchify — social watch-party + taste app  
**Live (soft launch):** https://watchify-web-9rx1.onrender.com  
**One-liner:** Discord has voice. Watchify has playheads + taste across the streaming apps you already pay for.

---

## Problem

Friends already watch the same shows on Netflix, Max, Disney+, and more — but the social layer is broken. Discord is for chat/voice, Teleparty-style tools are thin browser extensions, and streamers themselves don’t own cross-service friend graphs. People want to watch *together* without sharing passwords or waiting for group texts.

## Solution

Watchify is the **social layer on top of streaming**, not a Netflix replacement:

- **Parties** — synced rooms with presence, chat, and playhead coordination  
- **Own-account sync** — each person opens their own streaming app; Watchify deep-links + syncs social state  
- **Watchify Free** — real in-app synced playback for allowed free titles  
- **Taste** — recommendations from favorites and friends (For you, Watch Match)  
- **Profiles** — shareable identity, cosmetics, “what I’m watching”

**We do not** stream licensed Netflix/Max/Disney+ video inside Watchify.

## How it works

1. Sign up → **30-day Party trial** (hosting unlocked).  
2. Follow friends / share presence.  
3. Host or join a party → guests join via link.  
4. Paid titles: everyone uses their own subscription; Watchify handles the room.  
5. Free titles: Watchify syncs play/pause/seek in-app.  
6. Link services for badges + deep links; optional **Trakt** history import.

## Wedge vs Discord + Netflix

| | Discord | Netflix | Watchify |
|--|---------|---------|----------|
| Voice / chat | Strong | Weak | Chat + party rooms |

**Positioning:** Not “another streamer.” The social OS for watching together.

## Competitive honesty

- **Teleparty / Syncplay-class tools:** playhead sync niche; Watchify adds social identity, taste, and multi-service presence.  
- **Discord:** wins chat/voice; doesn’t own watch playheads or taste across streamers.  
- **Netflix Party legacy / streamer social:** locked to one catalog.  
- **Risk we own:** sync quality for paid apps is “good enough social,” not pixel-perfect DRM control — we say that out loud.
