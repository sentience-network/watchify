# Watchify Vision

## One-liner

**Watchify is the social layer for everything you watch** — presence, parties, and shared taste across every screen.

Tagline: **Watch together. Across every screen.**

## The billion-dollar insight

Streaming catalogs are commoditized. The moat is **cross-service social identity**: who you watch with, what’s live right now, and the taste graph that compounds with every friend you invite.

## Flywheel

1. **Watch** (on a linked service or Watchify Free)  
2. **Share** presence + activity (friends follow for free)  
3. **Invite** to a party (1-tap link)  
4. **Party** (chat / own-account sync / free synced playback)  
5. **More friends** join the graph  
6. **More watches** → better For You recommendations → more parties  

## Product modes (legal only)

| Mode | What it is | What it is not |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Social share | Metadata, badges, chat | Shared Netflix passwords |
| Own-account sync | Teleparty-style playhead + deep links | Proxying paid DRM video |
| Watchify Free | Hosted CC / PD / sample playback | Scraped Hollywood libraries |
| Screen share | Free/owned media only | Screen-sharing paid streamer apps |

## Content acquisition ladder

1. Public domain & Creative Commons  
2. Official trailers (YouTube embeds)  
3. AVOD partners (Tubi / Pluto / Plex-style)  
4. Aggregators (Filmhub, Quiver, etc.)  
5. Studio / streamer licensing deals  
6. Originals + UGC with DMCA  

## Path to scale

- **Free tier** → network effects (join parties, see friends watching)  
- **Plus** → unlimited lists + full service linking  
- **Party** → hosts unlimited live rooms (the monetization of social leadership)  
- **Future** → AVOD ads on free catalog, brand parties, licensed windows  

## What we never claim

We do not pirate, scrape DRM, or redistribute paid streams to non-subscribers. Social follow ≠ watching the film.
